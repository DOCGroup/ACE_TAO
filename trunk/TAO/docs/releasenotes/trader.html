TAO Trading Service Documentation

The TAO transient Trading Service implementation conforms to the Link Trader conformance criteria as detailed in the COS TradingObject Service specification. This document details how to use the TAO Trading Service from four perspectives:
  • as a importer bootstrapping to the Trading Service,
  • as a Service Offer exporter,
  • as an out-of-the-box server process,
  • as a collocated object.
This document assumes you are familiar with Trading Service concepts, such as "importer," "exporter", "service type", "service offer," and "dynamic property", as well as the roles of each of the Trading Service's interfaces --- Lookup, Register, Admin, and Link (the TAO implementation doesn't currently support Proxy). I recommend reading the first two sections of the Trading Service specification (about 20 pages). This document has the following layout:
