The Way of DOC

To build an effective systems research group, it's essential to develop a culture that is supportive to software development and software system researchers and developers. The following are some of the key precepts that underly the culture of the DOC research group:
  • Work hard
  • Have fun
  • Be creative
  • Learn new skills
  • Teach your skills to others
  • Value the contributions of team members
  • Write papers
  • Read papers
  • Give presentations
  • Respond to ACE users questions
  • Delegate responsibility
  • Accept responsibility
  • Play chess
  • Play chess after being awake all night
  • Make paper airplanes
  • Drink Nestea
  • Watch South Park
  • Juggle and bounce balls
  • Swear under your breath at C++
  • Fix at least two bugs every day
  • Create at least one bug every day (and then fix it ;-))
  • Fix your own bugs
  • Swear at other people's bugs.... but also fix them
  • Move one step closer to world domination every day
